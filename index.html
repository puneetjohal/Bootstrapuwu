Hello all! We hope that our webpage is able to leave you with a better understanding of the design capabilities of the bootstrap framework. Here are a few things you can try:

  • Resizing the browser
  • Zomming in or out (CTRL + or CTRL -)
  • Clicking through the navbar
  • Hovering over the images below with your mouse
  • Clicking on the different buttons below

First, you can see that we used a Jumbotron here. To check this out, feel free to view the source code!

How to View Source
To view the source of a webpage, simply right click near where you want to view the source (perhaps the button above), and click "Inspect Element". Doing this will show you the source for that page and will even allow you to manipulate it!
